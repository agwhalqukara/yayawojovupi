Another in the fire lyrics

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

This article has another in the fire lyrics and also gives a summarized brief about the Hillsong United band

About

“Another in the Fire” is a song by Christian rock band, Hillsong United. The song was released on April 26, 2019 as part of their album “People”.

It talks about how God is always with us, even in our darkest moments when we feel like we are alone and no one understands what we are going through.

The lyrics also highlight the power of faith and how it can help us overcome any challenge we may face in life.

Overall, “Another in the Fire” is an uplifting and inspiring song that reminds us of the constant presence of God in our lives.
