Mandy Moore Dating Dawes Singer Taylor Goldsmith Post-Ryan Adams Split

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Is love in the stars once more for Mandy Moore? The “Candy” singer, who split from husband Ryan Adams after almost six years of marriage this past January, has been spending time with Dawes lead singer Taylor Goldsmith, and a source tells Us Weekly that they’re dating.

On Friday, July 24, Moore shared a photo on Instagram of the musician with his head bent down over a Scrabble board.

“Flashback to last week when my 52 point word befuddled both of us,” she captioned the shot, adding in an Emoji pink heart and the hashtag “fbf.”

Less than a week ago, Moore, 31, also shared snapshots from the sidelines of the band playing in Minneapolis and Fargo.

Dawes is currently touring to promote their fourth album, All Your Favorite Bands, which the group released earlier this year.

“Perfect Midwestern Summer evening with a stellar soundtrack c/o @dawestheband,” Moore captioned one photo.

Before her relationship and marriage to Adams, Moore dated tennis champion Andy Roddick, That ‘70s Show actor Wilmer Valderrama, and Garden State director Zach Braff.
