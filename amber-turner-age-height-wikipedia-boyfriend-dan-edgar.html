Amber’s Wardrobe: Fashion Entrepreneur

Amber’s entrepreneurial spirit led her to establish her own fashion line, “Amber’s Wardrobe,” on Depop. This platform allows her to sell her clothing collections and brands. Additionally, Amber has ventured into activewear, sharing fitness-related content on her social media channels.

Amber’s fame reached new heights when she appeared on the reality show “The Only Way Is Essex” (TOWIE). This show, which first aired on October 10, 2010, on ITVBe and ITV2 networks, provides viewers with an insight into the lives of Essex’s residents, including stars like Denise Van Outen, Chloe Sims, and James Bennewith.

Amber proudly serves as a brand ambassador for Envy Shoes UK, often featuring the brand on her social media platforms and linking to its official Instagram page.

Are Amber Turner and Dan Edgar Still Together?

Amber Turner and Dan Edgar, two prominent figures from “The Only Way Is Essex,” have recently parted ways, sparking rumors and questions about the status of their relationship. Let’s take a closer look at what has unfolded.

Amber Turner, with her substantial following of 843K on Instagram, took to her Instagram Stories to confirm the breakup. She addressed her followers, stating,

“I wasn’t going to post anything but due to speculation and questions I feel like I need to. Yes me and Dan have split.”

This announcement followed earlier hints of a breakup when fans noticed that Amber had posted pictures from a holiday in Dubai without Dan. As of now, Dan Edgar has not made any public statements regarding the breakup. However, actions often speak louder than words, and he has deleted all traces of their relationship from his Instagram profile.

James ‘Lockie’ Lock, a close friend of Dan, shed some light on the situation at the launch of his gymwear brand, ‘Levo.’ Lockie mentioned that Dan is doing

“as good as could be” given the circumstances and plans to meet up with him soon to offer support as a friend.

Lockie emphasized the importance of respecting their privacy during this challenging period, stating,

“I don’t get too involved, I’ve been at the receiving end when people get too involved and it’s not their business. I’ll see Dan at the weekend, I’m there for Dan as a mate.”

Amber Turner’s decision not to attend Lockie’s launch event, where they both have mutual friends, hints at a level of discomfort following the breakup becoming public knowledge. Lockie clarified that there is no animosity or awkwardness, but the situation is undoubtedly a sensitive one.

Amber and Dan’s relationship has been a rollercoaster ride, marked by on-and-off periods before officially going public as a couple. There was a brief split during which Dan briefly dated Chloe Sims. Last year, Amber also expressed doubts about their relationship.

Amber and Dan’s recent split comes after they purchased a house together and moved in with their dog, Oliver. While discussing their move in a video on the TOWIE Facebook account, Dan humorously mentioned that he sometimes questions why he’s with Amber, prompting playful banter between the couple.

As the breakup news continues to circulate, Amber and Dan’s representatives have been contacted for comment. The situation remains fluid, and fans are left wondering what the future holds for these two TOWIE stars.
