Kylie Jenner Sparks Second Pregnancy Rumor With This Announcement

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

In an unexpected twist of events, Kylie Jenner missed the Paris Fashion Week, as she has reportedly been hospitalized.

It is not known exactly what is wrong with the reality TV star at the moment, but reports indicate that she has been dealing with symptoms close to those of the flu. However, fans think she is pregnant with baby number 2.

Jenner was supposed to show up next to Kim Kardashian and Kendall Jenner, but those plans were canceled.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B21Wgc4HVmu/

Kylie missed the event entirely, even though she was also the artistic director of makeup for it, which has led to the disappointment of many of her fans.

Kylie was quick to issue a statement about the situation though and wanted to reassure her fans that she would do her best to get back on her feet as quickly as possible and resume her work.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B2yA3YvH6YI/

She added that she was heartbroken over having to miss the event in the first place, but noted that there was nothing she could do in this situation.

In the end, the most important thing for Kylie right now is to take care of her health and ensure that she pulls through this situation without any complications.

An official representative for the makeup mogul has confirmed that her condition is okay, and she will make a recovery soon.

However, for the time being, any work is out of the question for her, and she will have to stay in bed to deal with her health problems for some time.
