Who Is Shahzada Dawood Wife, Christine?

Shahzada Dawood, a prominent entrepreneur from Pakistan, is a key figure in the Dawood family business Group and holds the position of vice chairman at Dawood Hercules Corporation.

Joining the family enterprise in 1996, he quickly rose to prominence in the corporate world and contributed to the transformation of Pakistan.

Shahzada has played a vital role in diverse public-listed companies within the fields of energy, agri-nutrients, consumer goods, petrochemicals, and textiles.

His expertise lies in identifying growth opportunities and fostering innovation through strategic mergers, acquisitions, and divestitures.

The Dawood Hercules family is engaged in a wide range of business ventures.

Additionally, Shahzada serves as a shareholder director on the boards of Dawood Lawrencepur Limited and Engro Corporation Limited.

He oversees the technological initiatives of the Dawood Group, which includes ventures leveraging the Internet of Things to disrupt various industries.

Demonstrating his commitment to a future powered by renewable energy, Shahzada has invested in Reon, a renowned solar and storage solution provider.

Furthermore, he is dedicated to supporting educational initiatives as part of his philanthropic endeavors.

As a trustee of The Dawood Foundation, Shahzada actively contributes to the operation of educational institutions up to the university level.

These include prestigious establishments such as Dawood Public School, Dawood College of Engineering, and Karachi School of Business and Leadership.

Shahzada also holds board membership with the Engro Foundation, an organization striving to make a significant social and economic impact.

He advocates for inclusive business models that involve low-income areas in the development of value chains, aligning with the corporate objectives of the Dawood Group.

Shahzada Dawood Wife

Shahzada Dawood is happily married to his wife, Christine. The couple has enjoyed a long and fulfilling marriage.

Throughout their journey together, they have been blessed with two children, named Sulaiman and Alina.

Currently, the family resides in the United Kingdom, where they share their home with their beloved pets, a dog named Stig and a cat named Proteus.
