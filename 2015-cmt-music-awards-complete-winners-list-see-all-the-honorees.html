2015 CMT Music Awards Complete Winners List: See All the Honorees!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 2, 2024

A country affair! Country music's biggest stars hit Nashville, Tenn., on Wednesday, June 10, for the 2015 CMT Music Awards.

The star-studded affair at the Bridgestone Arena played host to a bevy of famous faces such as Carrie Underwood, Lady Antebellum, Zedd, Nikki Reed, and even Rumer Willis. The celebs mixed and mingled on the red carpet outside of the celebratory affair before taking in electric performances and accepting major awards.

Underwood, 32, started the night with the most nominations, even competing against herself in the Female Video of the Year category. Other nominees with multiple nods included Miranda Lambert, Florida Georgia Line, and Luke Bryan.

Check out the updating full list of winners below!
