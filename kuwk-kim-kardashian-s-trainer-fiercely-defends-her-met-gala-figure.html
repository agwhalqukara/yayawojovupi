KUWK: Kim Kardashians Trainer Fiercely Defends Her Met Gala Figure

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024

Kim Kardashian looked like out of this world while in attendance at the Met Gala and for some, that was a good thing while for others, not so much! The KUWK star showed off her very unique physique and fans were definitely split!

A part thought she looked stunning while others think she is really unnatural looking and worried Kim is setting more unattainable beauty standards for young girls out there.

Either way, she managed to turn heads at the event.

She showed up alongside her husband Kanye West and was wearing a figure-hugging dress designed by Mugler.

Haters were quick to accuse the reality TV star of extremely manipulating her waist, making it unnaturally tiny to fit into the unique gown.

That is when Kim’s personal trainer Melisa Alcantara clapped back, setting the record straight.

‘To make things clear. 1. This dress is indeed corseted BUT 2. Kim trains her a** off 6 days a f***ing week, she wakes up early AF and she is dedicated. 3. I paved the road but SHE did the work! MOST IMPORTANTLY I don’t give s**t about your opinions on her body, if you think it is fake or not! I see her every morning, I see her train and see her sweat and see all the work she does outside the gym and THAT is commendable!’ Savage!

Sure enough, Kim appreciated the passionate defense and reposted her trainer’s message on her own platform.

While chatting with E’s Zuri Hall, the star explained the inspiration for the stunning dress.
