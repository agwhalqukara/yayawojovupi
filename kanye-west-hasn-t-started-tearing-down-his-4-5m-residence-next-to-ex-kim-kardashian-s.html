Kanye West hasnt started tearing Down his $4.5m Residence Next To ex Kim Kardashians

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 29, 2024

He paid $4.5 million for the property next to his ex-wife Kim Kardashian, with intentions to ‘demolish’ it and build a new mansion.

Kanye West’s development plans appear to be on hold, according to TMZ.

The rapper, 44, has failed to obtain building permissions for the home, which the source attributes to him still completing architectural designs.
According to the website, his neighbours report they haven’t seen him.
Kanye bought the house in December to be near to Kim and their four kids.

When he purchased it, People said he intended to build it quickly.
Kanye’s new home is a wreck. Just for the location. It’s close to Kim’s. So he’s planning to start building as soon as possible,’ People reported in December.

He bought it, a source told The New York Post, “just to be near to his kids and be able to see them at any time.”

Kanye spent $4.5 million for the 1955 home, $421,000 above the asking price to avoid being outbid.

The property is 3,651 square feet with 5 bedrooms and 3.5 baths on 1.07 acres of land.

There is a huge outdoor pool and a horse corral with three stables.

He has openly stated his desire to reconnect with Kim, despite her relationship with Pete Davidson.

Kim paid $23 million for their LA house, including all furnishings, finishes, and art.

They bought it for $20 million in 2014, and her mom Kris Jenner stated it was worth $60 million in 2018.
Construction of his Malibu residence began in February 2022 for $57.3 million.

The artist gutted his beachfront home.

After seven years of marriage, Kim divorced Kanye in February 2021.

North, 8, Saint, 6, Chicago, 4, and Psalm, 2 are Kanye’s kids.
Kim filed for divorce in February 2021 and was legally single in March while they sorted out custody.

She stated this in an interview with Amanda Hirsch for her podcast Not Skinny But Not Fat.

‘We don’t really communicate right now, but I believe that’s acceptable sometimes,’ she remarked. ‘We’ll. We will. That’s me.’

‘I spent a decade with this individual, and I adore him. That won’t change, but that doesn’t imply they’re the perfect one for you,’ Kim added.

‘We spent around eight months without ever speaking to one other before the divorce,’ she added.
