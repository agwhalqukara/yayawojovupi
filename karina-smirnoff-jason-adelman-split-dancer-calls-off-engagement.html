Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, September 13, 2024

It's over. Engaged for less than two months, Karina Smirnoff and her fianc√© Jason Adelman have called it quits.

The former Dancing With the Stars pro, 37, has scrapped her plans to marry the actor, also 37. The American Dreams alum proposed to Smirnoff in mid-January, but their bliss did not last long. 

A source close to the couple confirms to Us Weekly that the split is official, and adds that it is "such a complicated mess."
