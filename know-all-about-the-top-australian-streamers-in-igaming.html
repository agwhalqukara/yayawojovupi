The female gamer is the second-most followed streamer on Twitch after Pokimane. She also involves herself with charity work like the 36-stream she held in the year 2020 to help those affected by the bushfire. She, along with other streamers, was able to raise over AUD$300,000 from their efforts.

LazarBeam mostly streams his content on YouTube. He had once held the title of one of the top-five most viewed content creators a few years back. Today, he still commands a large following and is the third-most viewed content creator in Australia. His content is also viewed across the world, especially by Fortnite lovers.

Fasffy is another social streamer who streams Fortnite and works with LoserFruit. The gamer comes from Victoria and streams for a few hours each day. She also travels to areas where there are events to stream them live on her channels. Like LoserFruit, her content is varied but basically centers around first-person games and mythical games. Fortnite recently appreciated her commitment to gaming by creating a skin bundle in her honor.

Crayator

Crayator is also a social streamer who mostly plays Fortnite. He has amassed over 800,000 followers on Twitch who follow his gaming methods and advice. When he is not playing Fortnite, he plays Just Chatting. While he streams most of his content from home, he occasionally travels around the world for gaming events such as Twitch Con. At some point in the recent past, he lived in the Australian Social Media Team House with a few other leading streamers, such as LoserFruit and Bazza Gazza. He later parted ways with LoserFruit but remained with Bazza Gazza.

PaladinAmber

PaladinAmber is another iGaming streamer that concentrates on Fall Guys and Fortnite. She already has over 100,000 followers on Twitch and streams varied content for her fans.
