The RHOBH star Kyle Richards stuns in an $800 floral minidress at brunch event in Sydney

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Kyle Richards has spent the past week in Sydney promoting the show. 

And on Thursday, the glamorous and age-defying 53-year-old wrapped up her whirlwind trip with an intimate influencer brunch at the Kimpton Margot hotel in the CBD.

Kyle, who is the sister of Kathy Hilton, looked incredible as she stepped out in a flirty floral minidress by the designer brand Zimmermann, which retails for around $800.

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills star Kyle Richards stunned in an $800 floral minidress on Thursday, as she wrapped up her whirlwind trip to Sydney with an influencer brunch. Pictured with host and influencer Deni Todorovic

Kyle’s frock featured a plunging neckline, balloon sleeves, a belt at the waist and a floaty skirt.  

The brunette beauty teamed the look with gold high heels and wore her long locks out and over her shoulders. 

Her makeup was complete with matte foundation, a smokey eye and a deep pink lip.

Speaking to The Kyle and Jackie O Show on Thursday, the RHOBH star said she’d like to see Brandi Glanville return to the hit TV franchise.

When asked who she’d like to see come back as a permanent Housewife out of Brandi, Dana Wilkey, or Lisa Vanderpump, Kyle was quick to pick Brandi.

‘She was always great TV,’ the Halloween star explained.

‘It was scary for me because she basically had nothing to lose and she would say anything,’ she continued.

‘But I feel like she actually has grown a lot, but yet she’s still herself. I’m actually friendly with her now, she’ll text me, always very sweet.

‘She’s been an incredible friend to my sister Kim. And I think she’s great TV.’

It comes after Kyle told Daily Mail Australia that she wants to see Teddi Mellencamp back on the hit Bravo series.

‘Teddi is an incredible person and an incredible friend and I think she is actually a great Housewife,’ she explained.

‘I think if she came back she’d be more comfortable just being more herself and she’d be amazing.’

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills is currently streaming on Hayu.
